PRO NINJA ACADEMY

Master the course like a professional athlete.

Elite Obstacle Navigation

Our Pro Ninja Academy is designed for those who want to take their skills to the next level. This program focuses on technical proficiency across various obstacles, including the warped wall, salmon ladder, and quintuple steps.

Participants will work with certified ninja coaches to improve their upper body strength, core stability, and overall agility. The program is structured into weekly modules, each focusing on a different set of challenges.

Program Highlights

  • ⏱️ 2 Hour Sessions
  • 🏋️ Personal Coaching
  • 🏆 Monthly Competitions
  • 👕 Official Academy Gear
