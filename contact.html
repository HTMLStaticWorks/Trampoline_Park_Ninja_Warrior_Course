Common Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit.

What should I wear? ▼
Comfortable athletic wear is recommended. We provide mandatory safety grip socks for all jumpers.
Do I need to book in advance? ▼
While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend booking online to guarantee your spot, especially on weekends.
Is there an age limit? ▼
We have zones for toddlers as well as extreme courses for adults. All ages are welcome!
Do you have lockers? ▼
Yes, we have secure lockers available for all guests to store their personal belongings safely.
