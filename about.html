OUR STORY

The Journey

How we became the city's #1 adventure destination.

2018

Founded with a single vision: to bring professional ninja training to families.

2020

Expanded to a 20,000 sq ft facility with the world's largest foam pit.

2022

Launched the Elite Warrior Academy and started hosting national competitions.

2026

Continuing to innovate with high-tech interactive obstacles and global expansion.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, high-energy environment where people of all ages can push their physical limits and discover their inner warrior.

Our Vision

To be the world's most influential adventure park brand, inspiring a global movement of active lifestyle and mental resilience.
